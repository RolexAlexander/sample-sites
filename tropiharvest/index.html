Rooted in Guyana, trusted worldwide

Founded in 1998 on the rich alluvial soils of the Mahaica-Berbice region, TropiHarvest Farms has cultivated a reputation for reliability, flavor, and export readiness. Our vertically integrated operations span over 1,200 acres with satellite farms strategically positioned for year-round supply continuity.

Sustainability practices

  • Rainwater harvesting and solar-powered irrigation.
  • Integrated pest management with biological controls.
  • Soil health monitoring and regenerative crop rotation.

Supply readiness

  • HACCP-compliant packhouse with cold chain integration.
  • Onsite USDA pre-clearance inspection support.
  • Export documentation team for CARICOM & North America.
