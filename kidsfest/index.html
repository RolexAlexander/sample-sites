About Kids Fest Collective

We choreograph unforgettable kids' celebrations without the chaos.

Founded by parents, educators, and designers, Kids Fest Collective delivers immersive celebrations that champion play, safety, and culture. From whimsical birthdays to campus takeovers, our team concept maps every detail so you can stay present in the magic.

Our Mission

To bring joy and wonder to every child's milestone through personalized, inclusive experiences that delight families.

Our Vision

To be Guyana's most trusted celebration partners, known for imaginative design and seamless execution.

Our Story

Milestones that shaped Kids Fest Collective.

2015

We launched Kids Fest to transform children's parties into immersive, hassle-free experiences for busy families.

2018

Expanded into school partnerships, themed festivals, and bespoke corporate family events.

2020

Celebrated our 500th event and introduced sensory-inclusive celebration kits for hybrid gatherings.

Today

We continue to innovate with creative labs, eco-friendly builds, and multilingual play captains across Guyana.
